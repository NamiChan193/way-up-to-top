Overview

The AI Companion is a personal digital assistant built using AI technologies to provide emotional, practical, and conversational support.

It is designed for individuals seeking companionship, productivity assistance, and mental wellness support.

This documentation outlines the key features, setup, and customization options for your AI Companion.

Unlike traditional assistants, this AI learns over time and adapts to your behavior.

It functions entirely offline or with optional cloud sync for advanced tasks.

Getting Started

To begin using the AI Companion, first download the official application for your operating system.

Once installed, launch the app and create your user profile with your name and preferred pronouns.

You will be asked to choose a voice for your AI Companion or allow it to generate one based on your preferences.

The onboarding process also includes setting a default mood mode, such as calm, cheerful, or neutral.

After onboarding, the AI Companion begins learning from your interactions and schedules daily check-ins.

installCompanion()
initializeProfile("yourName")
chooseVoice("neural-female-soft")
setDefaultMood("calm")
startLearningMode()

How to check out our companion:

  1. Download the app from the official website
  2. Create and personalize your profile
  3. Choose a voice and personality tone
  4. Set mood detection preferences
  5. Start interacting with the AI Companion

Core Functionalities

The AI Companion is designed to support users in various aspects of daily life through intelligent, adaptive interactions.

Core functionalities include mood detection, smart reminders, conversational memory, and personalized suggestions.

Users can ask the companion to help with tasks like journaling, breathing exercises, or motivational quotes.

The AI also tracks user sentiment over time, helping you notice emotional patterns and behaviors.

Integration with calendars and to-do lists makes the companion a useful productivity ally as well.

aiCompanion.remind("drink water", "every 2 hours")
aiCompanion.journal("Today I felt...")
aiCompanion.analyzeMood("past week")
aiCompanion.suggest("morning routine")
aiCompanion.talk("I'm feeling anxious")
  • Real-time mood recognition and feedback
  • Conversational journaling
  • Scheduled and context-aware reminders
  • Motivational and therapeutic dialogues
  • Integration with task managers and calendars

Privacy and Data Handling

Privacy is a core value of the AI Companion, with all personal data stored locally by default.

The AI does not upload your conversations to external servers unless cloud sync is explicitly enabled.

All emotional data, usage logs, and interactions are encrypted and accessible only to the user.

Users can delete specific memories or entire data histories at any time using the privacy settings.

Transparency reports are available, showing what data is used, when, and why.

enableCloudSync(false)
deleteMemory("May 5, 2025")
viewPrivacyReport()
exportData("backup.json")
clearAllInteractions()
  • Local-first data policy
  • Optional encrypted cloud sync
  • User-controlled data deletion
  • Data usage transparency
  • End-to-end encryption of conversations

Customization API

The AI Companion includes a lightweight API that allows users to customize its responses, triggers, and behaviors.

You can define new "skills" using simple JavaScript-like syntax, which the companion will recognize and integrate.

Each skill is composed of a name, a trigger, and a response or function to execute.

You can also modify or remove default behaviors and override personality traits using the same API.

All customizations are sandboxed and safe, with an option to preview before applying changes.
